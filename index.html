Our Services

What we do

We are ready to serve with our professional team along with our direct connections that helps you to keep your regulatory information updated, complete the project with appropriate way and suitable lead time.
Also, at Certoyd you can handle your MENA Approvals through one channel (Certoyd) without any worries about the regulatory difference difficulties.

Telecom RF Approvals

Telecom Authorities type approval certification for Radio and Telecommunications Terminal Equipment (“RTTE”).

Safety Approvals

Standardization Authorities product certification such as, ECAS LVE, ECAS RoHS, EESL, G-Mark, ..etc.

Shipment Approvals

Standardization Authorities shipment certification such as, SABER, TER/TIR, ..etc.

Regulations Consultancy

Checking products applicable/mandatory approvals, advising about specific regulations, ..etc.
